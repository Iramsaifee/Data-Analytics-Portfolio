# Projects

## 1. Retail Sales Performance Analysis

### Problem Statement
A retail organization is facing challenges in maintaining consistent profitability for its diverse product offerings, geographical markets, and customer segments. Although the organization is experiencing increased sales, certain orders or product segments are resulting in low or negative profitability. This may be due to high discounts, inefficient shipping options, or poor product segment performance.

The objective of this project is to analyze the sales performance of a retail organization to identify the key factors influencing profitability.

### Tools Used
- Python
- SQL
- Power BI

### Key Insights

1. Technology is the most profitable segment.

2. Furniture is contributing high sales but low profit.

3. Tables and Bookcases are contributing negative profit.

4. High discounts are negatively impacting the organization’s profitability.

5. The west region is contributing the highest sales and profit.

6. Sales are high in November and December.

7. Consumer segment is contributing the highest revenue.


### Business Recommendations

1. Discounts on products such as Tables and Bookcases, which are currently loss-making products, should be reduced.

2. Pricing strategy in the Furniture category needs to be optimized to achieve high profit margins.

3. More emphasis should be placed on growing sales in the West region, as it has the highest profitability.

4. Marketing efforts should be intensified before the holiday season to achieve high sales.

5. Discount strategies need to be optimized to achieve high sales as well as high profitability.
